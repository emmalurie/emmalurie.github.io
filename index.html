About Me

I am currently at third-year student studying Computer Science and Chinese Language and Culture at Wellesley College in Wellesley, MA, USA. I am passionate about leveraging technology to improve the world. Some of my favorite topics to explore are online misinformation, web literacy, social media platform algorithms, the advertising technology ecosystem, and online hate groups. Outside of the classroom and research, I spend my time listening to podcasts, reading as many books as possible, and volunteering in STEM outreach efforts.
