Emma Lurie

I'm a senior at Wellesley College studying Computer Science and Chinese Language & Culture. I am involved in research with the Wellesley Cred Lab. Previously, I worked as an undergraduate research assistant for the MIT Election Data and Science Lab.

Research

My favorite research topics to explore are web literacy, platform algorithms, online misinformation, the advertising technology ecosystem, and how to build more equitable sociotechnical systems.

Publications:

  • Lurie, Emma, and Eni Mustafaraj. "Investigating the Effects of Google's Search Engine Result Page in Evaluating the Credibility of Online News Sources." Proceedings of the 10th ACM Conference on Web Science. ACM, 2018.
  • Lurie, Emma*, Annabel Rothschild*, and Eni Mustafaraj. "How the Interplay of Google and Wikipedia Affects Perceptions of Online News Sources." Computation + Journalism 2019. Miami, FL.
