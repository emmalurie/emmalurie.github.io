Emma Lurie


I'm a third year PhD student at the UC Berkeley School of Information advised by Deirdre Mulligan. I am a public-interest technologist researching online election infrastructure, automated and crowdsourced fact-checking, and online reputation management.

I have a BA in Computer Science and Chinese Language & Culture from Wellesley College. Previously, I've worked as research assistant at the Wellesley College Cred Lab and the MIT Election Data and Science Lab, as well as a Civic Digital Fellow at the U.S. Census Bureau.
